Pavers are often a better choice than poured concrete because they offer more design flexibility, easier repairs, and a more refined finished look. Individual pavers can also be replaced without removing the entire surface.
For most projects, high-quality concrete pavers are one of the best choices for durability, appearance, and long-term performance. In some cases, natural stone or permeable pavers may also be a smart fit based on the style of the home and the needs of the site.
Paver patio cost depends on size, material selection, grading, site access, drainage needs, and overall project complexity. For homeowners in Winston-Salem, pricing can also vary based on the property layout and whether the patio is part of a larger outdoor living plan.
