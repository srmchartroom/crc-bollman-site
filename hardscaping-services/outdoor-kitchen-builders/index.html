Most homeowners considering an outdoor kitchen build aren't wanting just a fancier grill.

The Design-Build Process

What the Outdoor Kitchen Design-Build Process Looks Like

Every outdoor kitchen project starts with the same question: what will make the most sense for the way you want to use the space? That answer shapes everything from layout and appliances to materials, surrounding hardscape, lighting, and drainage.

As experienced outdoor kitchen designers and outdoor kitchen contractors, BOLLMAN approaches each project with both design judgment and construction discipline — because a kitchen that looks good still has to function well, hold up outdoors, and fit the property around it.

Features Often Planned Alongside the Kitchen

  • Built-in grills and full appliance packages
  • Prep counters, storage, and serving areas
  • Dedicated islands, bars, and seating walls
  • Patio expansions and paver work
  • Landscape and task lighting
  • Integrated seating and masonry elements
  • Drainage improvements and utility coordination
  • Fire features, including fire pits, fire tables, and full outdoor fireplaces
