Landscape and Hardscape Design,
Beautifully Built + Built to Last

Plenty of outdoor projects look good on day one... but far less still perform years later. At BOLLMAN Landscape, we believe your outdoor space should do both.

As a landscape and hardscape design company owned and operated by a licensed General Contractor, we understand that extraordinary results come from more than surface-level style. They come from thoughtful planning, structural integrity, drainage expertise, material performance, code awareness, and a build process that respects your home as much as the final result.

That's the BOLLMAN difference.

For homeowners investing seriously in their property, that difference matters.

Bollman Landscape provides landscaping and hardscaping services in Stokesdale, Greensboro, Winston-Salem, Kernersville, High Point, Summerfield, Oak Ridge, and Jamestown, North Carolina. Services include landscape design, paver patios, retaining walls, outdoor kitchens, inground fiberglass pools, outdoor living spaces, pergolas, pavilions, gazebos, fire features, water features, walkways, driveways, excavating, grading, hydroseeding, and landscape renovations.
